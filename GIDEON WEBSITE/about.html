Meet the Founder

Gideon Olukanni

Gideon Olukanni is an aspiring neuroscientist and passionate science learner currently in his final year of secondary school (SS3) as at 2026. With a deep curiosity about how the brain shapes thought, behavior, and human potential, he has committed himself to studying neuroscience and building a future in brain research.

Driven by a strong desire to understand the mysteries of the mind, Gideon founded Neurovascular Dynamic Lab as a platform to share insights about the brain in ways that are simple, engaging, and accessible.
